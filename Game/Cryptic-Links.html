How to Play

Aim of the Game

The aim of the game is to guess all 4 words in as few attempts as possible. Each word (except the first) is related in some fashion to its predecssor. They can be an examples of, types of, a synonym for, or an alternate meaning etc

How do the clues work?

Clues tend to vary a bit. A common thing to look out for are homophones (puns) and concatenating (joining) two words together. For example, for a Christmas clue, "A simpson requesting money from the bank" would be "Home Alone" as a pun on "Homer Loan".
